Biography: Houwen is a senior researcher working on computer vision and deep learning at Microsoft Research as of 2018. Before that he was a senior engineer at Qualcomm AI Research. He received Ph.D. from NLPR, Instituation of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2016. From 2015 to 2016, he worked as a visiting research scholar at Temple University. His research interest includes neural architecture search and design, video object tracking, segmentation and detection, video moment localization, saliency detection, etc.
